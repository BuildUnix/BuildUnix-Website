# BuildUnix Hero Video Prompt

Only the hero video is required now. The remaining product visuals are generated in code.

## Google AI Studio / Veo Prompt

Generate a 20-30 second seamless looping 4K cinematic hero video for a premium construction technology website.

Scene: a large-scale Indian residential construction site at dawn, starting from an elevated bird's-eye / drone-style view. The opening shot should reveal the full scale of the project: multiple concrete towers under construction, tower cranes, scaffolding, temporary site roads, material stacks, workers moving across zones, trucks, rebar yards, and active work areas. It should feel like a Rs 1,000+ Cr Indian construction project in Hyderabad or a similar Indian metro.

Camera movement: start with a slow aerial push-in from above, then gently transition into a lower cinematic angle near the structure. The movement should feel smooth, premium, and realistic, not fast or flashy. The loop should end in a composition that can blend naturally back into the opening.

Visual details: tower cranes rotating slowly, workers in orange and yellow safety vests, concrete mixers, slab areas, columns, rebar cages, scaffolding silhouettes, site equipment, dust in warm sunrise light, and organized but intense activity across the whole site.

Mood: industrial, premium, precise, large-scale, serious, and real. The feeling should be: India's skyline is being built here.

Color grade: warm amber sunrise highlights, charcoal shadows, natural concrete tones, subtle dust haze. Avoid cool blue corporate tones.

Requirements: realistic construction footage, not a 3D render. Indian construction site context, not Western or Middle Eastern. No text. No logos. No UI overlays. No audio. No overly clean stock-video look. No unsafe accident-like scenes. Aspect ratio 16:9. Resolution 4K. Seamless loop.
